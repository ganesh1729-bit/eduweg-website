Frequently Asked Questions

Do you guarantee a visa?

No, and no honest consultant can. The final decision is always made by the German embassy or consulate. Our job is to ensure your application is 100% perfect and meets all requirements to maximize your chances of success.

Why are your prices lower than other big companies?

We are a small, passionate team with low overheads. We don't spend heavily on advertising. Our focus is on providing high-quality, genuine help, not on maximizing profit from every student. We believe in fair pricing.

What is the "Germany Connect" call?

It's a unique video call with our co-founder who lives in Germany. You can ask practical questions about finding accommodation, city registration (Anmeldung), opening a bank account, or just daily life in Germany. It's designed to give you peace of mind before you travel.
